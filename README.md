# Retail Store Sales Dashboard 📊

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **Retail Store Sales Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends across different regions and customer segments.

The dashboard transforms raw retail sales data into actionable business insights, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing regions and products.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Monitor monthly sales and profit trends.
* Provide actionable recommendations for business growth.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales: **$2.30M**
* Total Profit: **$286.4K**
* Total Quantity Sold: **38K**

### Visualizations Included

* Sales by Region
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Monthly Profit Trend
* Quantity by Category
* Quantity by Customer Segment
* Top Customers by Orders
* Sales & Profit by Sub-Category
* Quarterly and Category Filters

---

## Key Insights

### Regional Performance

* West region generated the highest sales revenue.
* South region recorded the lowest sales performance.

### Sales Trends

* Strong sales growth observed during Q4.
* November and December were the highest-performing months.

### Product Performance

* Office Supplies accounted for the majority of quantity sold.
* Phones emerged as the top revenue-generating sub-category.

### Customer Analysis

* Consumer segment contributed over 50% of total sales volume.
* A small group of repeat customers generated a significant number of orders.

### Profitability

* Profit trends closely followed sales trends, indicating healthy business performance during peak sales periods.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory before peak sales seasons.
2. Focus marketing efforts on underperforming regions.
3. Promote high-performing product categories such as Phones and Accessories.
4. Implement loyalty programs for repeat customers.
5. Explore opportunities to expand corporate customer sales.
